ONEPIECE

One Piece, created by Eiichiro Oda, is a widely popular Japanese manga and anime franchise that has captivated audiences worldwide. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with a rubber-like body (due to eating a Devil Fruit), and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, as they sail the Grand Line in search of the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece"
